Sports & Outdoors

Not just hobbies, but a reflection of a team-oriented, physically committed, and challenge-driven mindset.

Field Hockey

Almost two decades of dedication to field hockey, including selection for the Rheinland-Pfalz-Saar State Team (2010–2016) and competitive play at Bundesliga level with Bremer HC. This experience instilled a profound understanding of team dynamics, discipline, and strategic decision-making under pressure. Hockey remains my sport of choice. The combination of technical skill, tactical awareness, and high-speed teamwork is where I thrive.

Martial Arts: Jujutsu Do & Combat Karate

Training at green belt level in both disciplines, with the Sempai title in Jujutsu Do. For me, martial arts is about effectiveness and mental discipline: understanding the mechanics of self-defence, building resilience, and maintaining respect for the practice and those training alongside you. It's continuous work, not destination.

Hiking & Outdoors

The outdoors is where resilience is tested. My pursuits span extensive hiking in New Zealand and the harsh landscapes of Greenland, to conquering Rwanda's volcanoes. I co-founded the Beyond the Hills hiking group in Kigali, facilitating outdoor experiences and community building. In Rwanda, I also challenged myself beyond my comfort zone by running the 2026 Kigali Peace Marathon. It's not my natural distance, but a reminder that growth often comes from testing unknown limits.
