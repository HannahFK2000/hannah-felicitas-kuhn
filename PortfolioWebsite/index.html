Bridging theory and impact.

I study systems to solve problems where it matters most.

From biomimetics in Bremen to field research in the Arctic, and through three years working across cultural and technical contexts on three continents, I've learned that meaningful solutions come from understanding. Not from outside expertise imposed, but from working alongside people who know their own systems. My MSc in Medical Engineering at KTH builds the technical foundation to engage in that kind of partnership work, across whatever contexts prove interesting.

Curiosity and resilience open doors. Understanding a place deeply enough to contribute meaningfully, and having the discipline to see things through. That's what creates real change.
